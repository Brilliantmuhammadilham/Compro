With a wide range of Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) products, Delta offers a competitive advantage to businesses seeking the best power solutions. Delta UPSs are designed to ensure companies can protect their critical applications by maintaining a steady flow of energy under harsh conditions. A UPS is an electrical device designed to provide emergency power even when other input power sources fail, and is an indispensable device in a variety of unrelated industries. These devices differ from standby generators or auxiliary or emergency power systems because, in the event of an input power failure, a UPS provides near-instantaneous protection for critical applications in the form of energy stored in batteries.
